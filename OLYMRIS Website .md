# Design Direction

1. Music is essential (ambient, cinematic, futuristic tone)
2. Motion should feel fluid and controlled, never aggressive
3. Large typography, strong negative space, minimal but precise copy
4. Do not over-explain — evoke, not instruct
5. First half: digital, abstract, technological
6. Second half: natural, organic, grounded
7. A clear transition must exist between both worlds
